Frequently Asked Questions


How does the app notify me about nearby hospitals?

The app uses GPS data and Google Maps API to track your location and provides notifications of nearby hospitals based on your current location.

How do I track the ambulance in real-time?

Once an ambulance is dispatched, the app provides real-time tracking via GPS, showing its location on the map along with the estimated time of arrival (ETA).

Can hospitals and drivers see my location?

Yes, with your consent, your location is shared with the hospital and ambulance driver to ensure a quicker response and accurate routing.

Does the app provide real-time traffic updates?

Yes, the app integrates with Google Maps to provide real-time traffic information to the ambulance driver, helping to optimize the fastest route.

How do I contact customer support for technical issues?

You can reach our customer support team through the contact form in the app or by calling our helpline at 123-456-7890 for immediate assistance.
